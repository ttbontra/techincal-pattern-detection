Pattern Overview

Name: Cup and Handle Pattern

Type: Continuation

Appearance: 'U' shaped cup with a slight pullback handle indicating consolidation before a breakout.

Significance: Predicts bullish trend continuation after consolidation and pullback.

Trading Strategy
Entry

Signal: Go long on price breaking handle's resistance with high volume.

Confirmation: Confirm with candle close above resistance.

Exit

Target: Measure cup depth, project upward from breakout for target.

Stop Loss: Set below handle's lowest point to limit loss.

Risk Management

Position Size: Adjust for risk not to exceed 1-2% of capital.

Profit Booking: Book partial profits at target, use trailing stop for remainder.

Considerations

Volume: Breakout volume spike confirms pattern.

Pattern Duration: Longer formation suggests significant breakout.

Market Context: Aligns best with overall bullish trend.
