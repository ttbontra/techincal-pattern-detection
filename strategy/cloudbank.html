Pattern Overview

Name: Cloudbank Pattern

Type: Reversal

Appearance: Small, clustered candles followed by a large breakout candle.

Significance: Indicates a potential trend reversal after consolidation.

Trading Strategy
Entry

Signal: Trade the breakout candle's direction with significant volume.

Confirmation: Additional candle close in breakout direction confirms entry.

Exit

Target: Use resistance/support or project cloud's height from breakout.

Stop Loss: Inside cloud's range, opposite breakout, to limit losses.

Risk Management

Position Size: Adjust for max 1-2% capital risk.

Profit Booking: Scale out at targets with a trailing stop for remaining position.

Considerations

Volume: Key for confirming breakout credibility.

Market Conditions: Align pattern with market sentiment for effectiveness.

Technical Indicators: Use RSI or MACD for additional breakout confirmation.
