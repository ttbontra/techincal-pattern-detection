Pattern Overview

Name: Mini Bear Flag Pattern

Type: Continuation

Appearance: Inverted bull flag with a quick decline ('pole') followed by a slight, often sideways, pause ('flag'). Short-term, indicating downtrend pause.

Significance: Suggests continued selling pressure, likely resuming the downtrend post flag breach.

Trading Strategy
Entry

Signal: Short on price drop below flag with significant volume.

Confirmation: Confirm with a candle closing below the flag to avoid false breakouts.

Exit

Target: Set target projecting pole's length downwards from the breakout.

Stop Loss: Just above the flag's highest point to limit losses.

Risk Management

Position Size: Adjust to keep potential loss under 1-2% of capital.

Profit Booking: Book profits at stages or use trailing stop loss to secure gains.

Considerations

Volume: Breakout volume spike confirms pattern.

Quick Execution: Use fast tools for pattern identification and trade execution.

Market Sentiment: Ensure pattern aligns with market sentiment; cautious against bullish trends.
