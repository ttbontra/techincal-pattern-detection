Pattern Overview

Name: Double Bottom Pattern

Type: Reversal

Appearance: Two troughs at similar levels with a peak in between, after a downtrend. Confirmation when price breaks above the peak's resistance.

Significance: Indicates a downtrend reversal, showing reduced selling pressure and potential uptrend.

Trading Strategy
Entry

Signal: Go long on resistance break with high volume. Wait for a candle close above resistance for more confirmation.

Exit

Target: Measure pattern height and project from breakout for profit target.

Stop Loss: Below the higher trough or broken resistance to limit loss.

Risk Management

Position Size: Ensure potential loss doesn't exceed 1-2% of capital.

Profit Booking: Book partial profits at levels or adjust stop loss to protect gains.

Considerations

Volume: Volume increase on breakout confirms pattern and reversal.

False Breakouts: Confirm breakout and check market context to avoid false signals.

Market Conditions: More reliable with overall bullish sentiment and indicators.
