Pattern Overview

Name: Double Top Pattern

Type: Reversal

Appearance: Two peaks at similar levels with a valley in between, indicating exhaustion of an uptrend. Confirmed by a drop below the valley's support.

Significance: Signals an uptrend reversal to downtrend, with selling pressure overtaking buying.

Trading Strategy
Entry

Signal: Short on support break with high volume. Consider waiting for a candle close below support for extra confirmation.

Exit

Target: Set by projecting the pattern's height downward from the breakout point.

Stop Loss: Just above the lower peak or the support level to minimize losses on a trend reversal.

Risk Management

Position Size: Ensure loss risk does not exceed 1-2% of capital.

Profit Booking: Book partial profits at levels or adjust stop loss to protect gains.

Considerations

Volume: Increased volume on the breakout confirms the pattern and trade potential.

False Breakouts: Validate with confirmation and market analysis to avoid false signals.

Market Conditions: Double tops are most reliable with bearish market sentiment and indicators.
