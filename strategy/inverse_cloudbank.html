Pattern Overview

Name: Inverse Cloudbank Pattern

Type: Reversal

Appearance: High-volatility 'cloud' of erratic candles, followed by a strong breakout candle opposing the trend.

Significance: Indicates market indecision then a potential trend reversal after a breakout.

Trading Strategy
Entry

Signal: Long on bullish, short on bearish reversal after a decisive breakout with high volume.

Confirmation: Strengthen signals with RSI divergence or MACD crossover.

Exit

Target: Set using support/resistance near breakout or 'cloud' height projection.

Stop Loss: Just inside 'cloud' opposite the breakout.

Risk Management

Position Size: Adjust for max 1-2% capital risk.

Profit Booking: Book partial profits with trailing stop loss for remaining position.

Considerations

Volume: Essential increase on breakout confirms pattern.

Market Conditions: Trade within overall sentiment and economic event context.

False Breakouts: Wait for confirmation, consider market trend and volume.
